Frequently Asked Questions

What are the income requirements?

We typically require tenants to have a monthly income of at least 3 times the monthly rent. We consider all verifiable income sources including employment, alimony, trust payments, and housing vouchers.

What is your pet policy?

Most of our properties are pet-friendly with some restrictions. We allow up to 2 pets per household with breed restrictions for dogs. There is a $300 refundable pet deposit and $35/month pet rent per animal.

How long is the lease term?

Our standard lease term is 12 months, but we also offer 6-month leases with a 10% premium on monthly rent. Some properties may offer month-to-month options after the initial lease term.

What utilities are included?

This varies by property. Most include water and trash service. Some include all utilities. The listing details will specify exactly what's included for each property.

How do I schedule a viewing?

Click the "Schedule Tour" button on any property listing to see available time slots. You can choose between in-person or virtual tours for most properties.
